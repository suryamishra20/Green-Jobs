Practise Interview

This is your chance to practise before taking your real interview. This is an important step in the process so you can check to ensure your microphone or webcam are working properly. Make sure to play back your practice recording and if you do not hear the audio clearly or see yourself clearly in the playback, adjust your settings or call GreenJobInterview for assistance.

Keep in mind that your practice video will not be saved or submitted. Practice as much as you'd like and don't worry about "being perfect". Just be yourself and remember, this is your chance to bring your resume to life and let your personality shine through.
